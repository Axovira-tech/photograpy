The Photographer

Chasing
light across
continents

For over a decade, I've traveled the world with a single obsession: the way light transforms the ordinary into the eternal. Born from a background in fine art, my photography is a bridge between painting and reality.

Whether it's a wedding ceremony on the coast of Amalfi or a silent portrait session in a Rajasthan courtyard — I seek the quiet, honest moment. My philosophy is simple: remain unobtrusive, observe deeply, and let the authenticity of the moment dictate the frame.
